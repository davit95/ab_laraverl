Virtual Office Location Spotlight: Los Angeles

BUSINESS INFORMATION

Nicknamed the City of Angels, Los Angeles is a global center of international trade, culture, entertainment, media, technology, fashion, sports, education, and business. The city is home to renowned institutions that span a wide range of cultural and professional industries and it represents one of the most significant economic engines within the U.S. Ranked the world's third wealthiest and fifth most powerful /influential city, as well as the third largest economic center in the world, Los Angeles provides an ideal location for virtual office space, business centers, executive office space and commercial real estate opportunities for the mobile entrepreneur and Alliance Virtual Offices offers a variety of prime Los Angeles virtual office space from which to choose. From the Howard Hughes and AT&T Centers to locations on Wilshire Boulevard and in the U.S. Bank Tower, Alliance Virtual Offices has got Los Angeles virtual office spaces covered.

GENERAL LOCATION INFORMATION

With a population of nearly four million, Los Angeles is the most heavily populated city in California and the second most populated in the country (following New York City). LA has an area of 468.67 square miles and is situated in Southern California. Los Angeles is the hub of the greater Los Angeles-Long Beach-Santa Ana metropolitan statistical area, and is one of the most populated metro areas in the world (second largest in the U.S.). As the seat of Los Angeles County, LA is the most populated and one of the most ethnically diverse cities in the country. The whole of the Los Angeles area has been identified as the most diverse of the United States' largest cities. As Hollywood's home base, Los Angeles is also referred to as the "Entertainment Capital of the World," leading the globe in motion pictures, television and stage production, video games, as well as music production. The great influence of the entertainment industry to the City of Los Angeles has resulted in many celebrities choosing to call the L.A. area home.

By: Monica Ochoa
